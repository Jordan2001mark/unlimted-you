What the App does.

Unlimited You provides its users with unlimited access to the internet through tunnelling your device thus putting you in a zone where you can access the internet without detection from your service providers.
This is achieved through connectimg you to a Virtual Private Network commonly known as VPN.

How the App works.

  • Sign up through the website using your email account.
  • Comfirm your account through a link sent to your email address.
  • Download the app.
  • Open the app and sign in with your account
  • Subscibe for any of our packages or use your Unlimited Now.
    Subscription can be done through our website.
  • Click connect on the app and enjoy unlimited internet connection.

Note that you need to have access to a data enabled SIM card but without a valid data plan. Or you can use wifi connection even though it has no internet connection.

How to Connect the App.

If you are in Uganda, Airtel Users must first pay the OTT tax in order to acquire the data connection.

MTN Users require nothing to connect to the internet, its just easy.

For other service providers,we have not yet confirmed if it works but for MTN and Airtel Users ,we have confirmed that the App works.

How to make a payment.

  • Sign in with your account.
  • Go to My Account and click on purchase a package.
  • Choose the package to acquire.
  • Choose a payment method.
  • Confirm payment to activate the package of your choice.
